Got Questions?

FAQ

Where does the money go?

+

100% of proceeds from ticket sales and donations go directly to Transcend Sarcoma, a charity dedicated to raising awareness and funding research into sarcoma. This is a community-driven event — every entry, every donation, every penny makes a difference.

Is the rooftop party included with my ticket?

+

Yes. The Level 16 rooftop finish party is included with every ticket — Full Challenge, Marathon + Murph, Half Marathon, and Murph Only. You can also buy a standalone rooftop party ticket if you just want to come and celebrate.

Do I need to run the full 84km?

+

No. Choose your level — a half marathon (21km), the full marathon + Murph (42km), the Murph workout only, or even just the rooftop party. There's also a donate option if you can't make it but want to support the cause.

What is the Murph workout?

+

The Murph is a legendary CrossFit Hero workout: 1 mile run, 100 pull-ups, 200 press-ups, 300 air squats, then another 1 mile run. It's performed at Newwave CrossFit Byfleet with their community. All fitness levels welcome — scale as needed.

Where does the event start and finish?

+

Both start and finish are at Level 16, Yellow Park in Wapping, London. The turnaround point is Newwave CrossFit on Redhill Road, Byfleet, Surrey.

Will there be hydration and support on the route?

+

Yes. Checkpoints with hydration and nutrition will be positioned along the route, including at Sheen Gate (Richmond Park) and Newwave CrossFit Byfleet. Full details will be shared closer to the event.

Can I combine segments or add merchandise to my ticket?

+

Yes. During checkout you can select multiple segments and add merchandise to your booking. You can also purchase merch separately from the online store.

What's included in the rooftop party?

+

The rooftop finish party at Level 16 includes access from 17:00, pizza, a welcome drink, and sunset views over the London skyline. Additional drinks available at the bar.
